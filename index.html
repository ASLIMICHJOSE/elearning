What is eLearning?

eLearning refers to the use of electronic media and information and communication technologies (ICT) in education. It allows students to learn at their own pace, from anywhere, and at any time, breaking geographical and time barriers.

Why is eLearning Important?

Accessible

eLearning can be accessed from anywhere in the world, making it easier for anyone to get an education regardless of location.

Cost-Effective

Compared to traditional classroom-based education, eLearning offers a more affordable alternative without the need for expensive materials or commuting costs.

Flexible Learning

eLearning allows students to set their own pace, meaning they can review lessons as needed and take their time with challenging material.

Engaging

Interactive content, quizzes, and videos make learning more enjoyable and effective, keeping students engaged in their studies.
